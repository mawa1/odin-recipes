How to Make Pancakes

Serve these pancakes with butter and syrup, or up the ante with toppings such.
as sprinkles, chopped bittersweet chocolate, and sliced or chopped fruit for a
perfect breakfast.

  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 1/4 cup white vinegar
  • 2 1/2 cups flour
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 tablespoon butter, softened, or as needed, for greasing, plus more for serving
  • toppings of choice, e.g. maple syrup or fruit, for serving
